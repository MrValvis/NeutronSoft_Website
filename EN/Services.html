Few of our under construction and complete project's

Estia

The ESTIA application objective is the electronic management of the renting houses/sites and the monitoring of renter obligation's. This application offer us instant overview of our renter(s) history and paying obligations without the need to look past and current bills and other papers than can cause confusion.

This application is build using C# and SQLServer in future we will add : 1) installer and unistaller for the application. 2) English language support. -Currently: the prototype is working from 25/01/2020

E-Ticketing

The E-ticketing application is underconstruction having as main purpose the instant communication with the IT helpdesk department for small-large size company. The application will help in archiving and maintaining a history for the users, also it will help in the proper division of workload and the monitoring of task's by the supervisors. Also the users can send their "problem-help" ticket even if their helpdesk colleague are not there.

Application is build in C# and SQLServer.
