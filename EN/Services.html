Estia

The ESTIA application objective is the electronic management of the renting houses/sites and the monitoring of renter obligation's. This application offer us instant overview of our renter(s) history and paying obligations without the need to look past and current bills and other papers than can cause confusion.

This application is built using C# and SQLServer in future we will add : 1) installer and uninstaller for the application. 2) English language support. -Currently: The prototype is in Alpha stage testing and working since 25/01/2020

E-Ticketing

The E-ticketing application is under construction having as main purpose the instant communication with the IT helpdesk department for small-large size company. The application will help in archiving and maintaining a history for the users, also it will help in the proper division of workload and the monitoring of tasks by the supervisors. Also, the users can send their "problem-help" ticket even if their helpdesk colleague are not there. Application is built on C# and SQLServer.

InMail

InMail this application was designed for the needs of marketing client , the app main concept is of a mail client that can only send e-mails. The functions that app is offer are:

  • Messenge and attachment sending.
  • Single or multiple recipients.
  • Show or hide emails of multiple recipients (cc/bcc).
  • You can use custom email without the need to exist.
  • Save of the email recipient's

This app is used for targeted marketing

This program on build on C# and SendGrid API.
